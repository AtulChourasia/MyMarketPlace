Welcome to my Full Stack MERN Application.
To run this app in your local machine follow steps below :-
-firstly make sure you have Mongo ,Node & React installed on your system globally amd your mongoServices are upp and running.
-clone or download my lepo to your local machine.
-open the projfrontend in terminal and type "npm install" all the required modules will be installed.
-open the projbackend in terminal and type "npm install" all the required modules will be installed.
-then again type "npm start" in the terminal on both folders.
-your browser will automatically start and my application will be running.
